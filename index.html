Analysis for my personal website by Google Analytics

HKBU JOUR2106 Homework6 by Lee Yee Man, Jam

How many people go to the website?



data analysis

The Google Analytics code was installed to my homepage on 1 April and the statistics was counted till 30 April for the homework 6.
I promoted my homepage to my friends through Facebook on 4th and 8th April respectively.
Obviously, these two days recorded the highest number of people clicked and read my homepage.
There are 86 people on 4th and 36 people on 8th April.
It shows that the first promotion is more effective than the second one. People usually pay higher attention to the first promotion.
